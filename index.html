The new Corvette C8 2021 Stingray is a dream that is within reach. At just $59,995 MSRP (base model), the new 2021 Mid-Engine Corvette Stingray sports a powerful 495 horsepower midengine, an impressive 2.9 seconds 0-60 time, and a sleek, stunning look that would make any head turn. This year, Chevrolet has an awesome and advanced visualizer that lets you build your own 2021 model.

Latest features of the 2021 Corvette:

  • 2021 base coupe model remains at the same price of the 2020 Corvette: $59,995
  • Two exterior new colors: Red Mist Tintcoat and Silver Flare Metallic
  • Two new interior color schemes: Grey and Yellow
  • Apple CarPlay and Android Auto now come as standard features
  • Models can now be ordered with magnetorheological dampers option without Z51 package
  • New Full Length Dual Racing Stripe in blue, orange, red, or yellow colors
